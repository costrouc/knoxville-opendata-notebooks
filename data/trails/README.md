# Description

Public-access, multi-use trails within Knox County provided by City of
Knoxville, Knox County, Town of Farragut and other entities such as
Legacy Parks and TWRA. This dataset contains paved greenways, foot
trails and shared use trails that may permit walking or biking. Urban
Wilderness trails are included.

Data for the City of Knoxville and Knox County is considered
authoritative and is reflects the best-available information about
facilities operated by these entities.
